FORMAT RULES:

1. **Text Fields:**
   Type "%FieldName%" into "FieldName"
   
2. **Dropdown Fields:**
   Select "%FieldName%" from Dropdown "FieldName"
   (Note: Capital D in "Dropdown")
   
3. **Textarea Fields:**
   Fill textarea "%FieldName%" with "FieldName"
   
4. **Buttons:**
   Click "ButtonName"
   
5. **Wait Statements:**
   Wait for 1 seconds
   (After EVERY action)
   
6. **CSV Placeholders:**
   %FieldName% (no quotes inside percent signs)
   
7. **Comments:**
   Use # for commented out steps
   Example: #Select "%Toolkits%" from Dropdown "Toolkits"
